About Us

Welcome to Mashariki Limited
Who we are

Founded in 2000 and based in Nairobi (Kenya) and Minneapolis, (Minnesota) Mashariki Limited operates through three business divisions: Transport, Supplies and Information Services & Technology (IST). Our mission is to become the company of choice to all our new and returning clients for all the products and services we offer. Our role is to provide a full range of goods supplies, regional transportation & logistics services, and a full array of Information technology solutions. We strive to achieve this by facilitating efficient partnerships of diverse supply chain partners,form professional partnerships with reputable manufactures, providers and suppliers and efficiently execute delivery to enable our client dedicate their energy towards the core aspects of their business. Our strength is rooted in listening to our clients and adapting our products and services to meet their objectives by continuously evaluating, innovating, and upgrading technology and processes to eliminate faults and attain maximum efficiency.
